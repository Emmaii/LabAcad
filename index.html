What is Trading?

Trading means buying or selling something in the market in order to make a profit from price movement. In this course, we focus on Gold.

  • Prices move up and down
  • Traders try to catch those moves
  • Good risk control matters more than guessing
🪙

What is Gold / XAUUSD?

Gold is the market we will study. On TradingView it is usually shown as XAUUSD, which means Gold priced in U.S. Dollars.

  • XAU means Gold
  • USD means U.S. Dollar
  • This is the chart you will use in the course
📱

What is TradingView?

TradingView is the charting platform we use to look at the market. It works on phone and on the web.

  • Download for iPhone
  • Download for Android
  • Open TradingView on Web
⚙️

How to Open the Gold Chart

Once TradingView is open, search for the Gold chart and select the correct feed so the chart appears properly.

  • Open the search bar in TradingView
  • Type "XAUUSD"
  • Select a broker feed such as OANDA:XAUUSD
  • Your Gold chart is now ready
