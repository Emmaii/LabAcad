Trading Methodology

Two-part systematic approach to trading

1. Quantitative Bias

Objective analysis using only previous price data. EMA crossovers and statistical z-scores determine directional bias.

  • Causal analysis (no future data)
  • Rule-based, emotion-free
  • Bullish/Neutral/Bearish classification

2. Chart Execution

Disciplined entry and risk management within quantitative boundaries. Price action confirmation and precise position sizing.

  • Price action alignment
  • Strict risk management
  • 1-2% risk per trade

Core Principles

Rules Over Emotions

The system eliminates emotional decision-making. Follow the code, not impulses.

Risk First

Every trade begins with stop-loss placement. Risk defines position size.

Data-Driven

Decisions based on statistical analysis, not hunches or predictions.

Bias analysis script. Computes data and gives weekly bias using EMA crossovers and statistical z-scores.

Features:

  • Causal analysis (previous data only)
  • EMA 8 vs 21 crossover signals
  • 52-week rolling z-score of returns
  • Bullish/Neutral/Bearish classification
  • CSV output with confidence metrics
